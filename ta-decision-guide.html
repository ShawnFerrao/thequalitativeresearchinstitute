These are not different levels of skill. They are different analytic roles — each is appropriate in the right context.
Question 4
What matters most for demonstrating quality in your analysis?
Using the wrong quality criteria for your approach weakens your research. Inter-rater reliability, for example, is appropriate in Coding Reliability TA — but not in Reflexive TA.

The team structure and purpose of your research often determine which approach is practical and credible to your audience.
