Meet Claire

A researcher who has been in your shoes

Throughout my career, I've recognised a persistent and crucial need for more effective qualitative research training.

Despite receiving the Dean's Award for Outstanding Research Higher Degree Theses, my own PhD in Health Psychology highlighted how difficult it can be to navigate qualitative research without proper training. This inspired me to return to my teaching roots, where I began creating the resources and workshops I'd needed earlier in my career.

Today, I'm dedicated to demystifying the complexities of qualitative research and methodologies, ensuring they are clear and accessible for researchers at every stage of their career.

By supporting researchers to build their skills and confidence, I can ensure they're equipped to produce valuable projects and effectively contribute to their fields.
