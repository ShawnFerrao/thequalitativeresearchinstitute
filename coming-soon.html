About

Helping researchers move forward with clarity and confidence

The Qualitative Research Institute offers expert training, consultancy, and support for researchers at every stage of their journey — from PhD candidates navigating their first qualitative project to experienced academics deepening their practice.

Led by Claire Moran PhD, our programs combine rigorous methodology with practical, hands-on learning. Whether online or face-to-face, we meet you where you are.

Based in Brisbane · Available Globally

Training delivered in Brisbane, Sydney, Melbourne, and online

Our workshops and programs are available face-to-face across Australia and via online delivery for researchers anywhere in the world. Wherever you are in your research journey, we have something for you.
